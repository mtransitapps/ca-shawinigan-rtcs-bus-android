This app adds Shawinigan RTCS buses information to MonTransit.

This app provides the buses schedule.

RTCS buses serve Shawinigan in Quebec, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link https://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by the City of Shawinigan and Régie de Transport en Commun de Shawinigan (RTCS).
https://donnees-shawinigan.opendata.arcgis.com/

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-shawinigan-rtcs-bus-android

This app is not related with the City of Shawinigan and Régie de Transport en Commun de Shawinigan (RTCS).
